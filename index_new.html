What we do

Our CTO as a Service is designed exclusively for providing access to all the services rendered by a CTO to non-tech founders and help them kickstart their product development while they can focus on other non-tech aspects essential for making their idea a successful company.

We partner with founding teams as early as the idea stage and work closely with them through all stages of their product development lifecycle.

  • Concept Validation
  • Visual & UX Design
  • Technology Stack Selection
  • Idea Proof of Concept
  • MVP Feature Selection
  • MVP development
  • Product Strategy
  • Product Management
  • Data Science

How we do it

Once we understand your long term vision, critical needs and short term goals, we onboard a CTO and spin-out a dedicated team of techies from our pre-vetted talent network to help you quickly and iteratively design, develop, and deploy your product.

All you need to do to get started is to subscribe to our service with fixed monthly or weekly installments. Oh, and did we mention the first week is on us!
